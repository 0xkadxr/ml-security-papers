# Relevant Conferences & Workshops

A list of academic conferences and workshops that regularly feature ML security and AI safety research.

---

## Top-Tier Security Conferences

### IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy (S&P)
- **Website:** https://www.ieee-security.org/TC/SP/
- **Frequency:** Annual (May)
- **Relevance:** Premier security conference with increasing coverage of ML security topics, adversarial ML, and LLM vulnerabilities.

### USENIX Security Symposium
- **Website:** https://www.usenix.org/conferences/security
- **Frequency:** Annual (August)
- **Relevance:** Top security venue with strong ML security track. Covers practical attacks and defenses on deployed systems.

### ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security (CCS)
- **Website:** https://www.sigsac.org/ccs.html
- **Frequency:** Annual (November)
- **Relevance:** Broad security conference with growing AI/ML security coverage. Hosts the AISec workshop.

### AISec Workshop (@ CCS)
- **Website:** Co-located with ACM CCS
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Dedicated workshop on AI and ML security. Many influential prompt injection and adversarial ML papers debut here.

---

## ML and AI Conferences

### NeurIPS (Neural Information Processing Systems)
- **Website:** https://neurips.cc/
- **Frequency:** Annual (December)
- **Relevance:** Top ML conference with safety workshops and tracks. Many foundational adversarial ML and alignment papers published here.

### NeurIPS ML Safety Workshop
- **Website:** Co-located with NeurIPS
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Dedicated workshop focused on ML safety, robustness, and alignment research.

### ICML (International Conference on Machine Learning)
- **Website:** https://icml.cc/
- **Frequency:** Annual (July)
- **Relevance:** Top ML conference featuring adversarial robustness, safety, and alignment research.

### ICLR (International Conference on Learning Representations)
- **Website:** https://iclr.cc/
- **Frequency:** Annual (May)
- **Relevance:** Leading ML conference. Hosts many papers on adversarial examples, robustness, and LLM safety.

## NLP Conferences

### ACL (Association for Computational Linguistics)
- **Website:** https://www.aclweb.org/
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Top NLP venue. Increasing coverage of LLM safety, prompt injection, and red-teaming research.

### EMNLP (Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing)
- **Website:** https://www.aclweb.org/
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Major NLP conference with growing LLM security and safety tracks.

### NAACL (North American Chapter of ACL)
- **Website:** https://naacl.org/
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Strong NLP venue with increasing AI safety and security content.

---

## Specialized Safety and Security Conferences

### IEEE Conference on Secure and Trustworthy Machine Learning (SaTML)
- **Website:** https://satml.org/
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Dedicated conference on trustworthy ML. Covers adversarial robustness, fairness, privacy, and security of ML systems. Relatively new but quickly becoming a key venue.

### AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence
- **Website:** https://aaai.org/
- **Frequency:** Annual (February)
- **Relevance:** Broad AI conference with safety and security tracks. Features adversarial ML and AI safety research.

### AAAI Workshop on Artificial Intelligence Safety (SafeAI)
- **Website:** Co-located with AAAI
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Dedicated workshop on AI safety topics including alignment, robustness, and security.

---

## Industry and Policy Conferences

### DEF CON AI Village
- **Website:** https://aivillage.org/
- **Frequency:** Annual (August)
- **Relevance:** Practical AI red-teaming and hacking. Hosts the AI Red-Teaming Challenge and hands-on workshops.

### RSA Conference
- **Website:** https://www.rsaconference.com/
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Relevance:** Industry security conference with increasing AI security content and tracks on LLM risks.
